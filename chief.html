Gyaseman Subchief

Role & Responsibilities

The Gyaseman Subchief holds a distinguished position within the Amanokrom traditional hierarchy. As a senior divisional chief, he supports the Omanhene in governance, administration, and the protection of ancestral traditions.

He serves as a custodian of sacred regalia and oversees important customary rites and ceremonial functions, ensuring the continuity of royal heritage.

Through wisdom, diplomacy, and unwavering dedication, the Gyaseman Subchief strengthens unity, stability, and development within the Amanokrom community.
